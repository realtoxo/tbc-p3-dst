# Sim vs Video Corroboration

This document compares the Phase 3 DST sim outputs against the class-specialist video/transcript layer.

## Verdict

The videos broadly corroborate the priority bands, but not every exact trinket-pair detail. Rogue, Fury, Ret, and Enhancement survive the cross-check cleanly. The unsettled areas are Hunter second-trinket ordering, Fury's no-Warglaives weapon baseline, Arms support, and any claim based on raid utility rather than personal DPS.

## Main Conflicts And Gaps

| Issue | Video layer | Sim layer | Status |

## LC Interpretation

- The video layer supports the sim's high-confidence DST pool: Rogue, Fury, Ret, Enhancement, and Survival.
- The P3 Hunter exact second trinket is a real conflict: the sim likes Brooch more than Madness when paired with DST.
- The Fury conclusion is usable only under the documented no-Warglaives weapon baseline.
- The sim does not measure Expose Weakness, Blood Frenzy, Ret utility, Enhancement utility, or Feral utility. Treat raid utility as a separate LC layer.
